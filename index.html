Sri Lakshmi Venkateshwara Woodworks & Interior Works has been serving Thamballapalle and surrounding areas for over 20 years. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name for quality woodwork, furniture, and interior solutions.

Under the expertise of K. Samba Siva Reddy, we take pride in delivering quality products at fair prices. From raw materials to finished furniture, from simple repairs to complete interior works - we do it all with dedication and craftsmanship.
